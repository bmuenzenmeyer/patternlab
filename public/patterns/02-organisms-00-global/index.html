I spend my working days in ux/ui design and my waking nights maintaining the node port of Brad Frost and Dave Molsen’s Pattern Lab and moonlighting as Crunchy Owl, a small studio. I am interested in the intersection of performance and responsive design to craft better things that reach more people. I live in Northeast Wisconsin with my wife, young son, 2 cats and 3 coffee makers. When not online, I am digging up the yard, wearing an afghan, talking about something I heard on public radio, dressing in grayscale, or pretending to play the piano. The best way to reach me is on Twitter @bmuenzenmeyer.
